About Us





    Shri Vidhya Gyan Ganga Private ITI -

    Shri Vidhya Gyan Ganga Private ITI is a best in class and rapidly growing ITI group in Madhya Pradesh. We started in 1999 with a motto of nourishing young minds by providing high class training to master a trade and be Industry ready. At Satpuda ITI, we are committed to develop the youth by equipping them with employable and entrepreneurial skill sets suitable to the industry.

    Since last 15 years we are working with same enthusiasm and determination to reduce the skill gap between industrial requirement and skill. We Started from Garra-Balaghat and had expanded while currently operating 4 ITI’s in Balaghat region, 2 in Chhindwara, Rewa, Betul, Multai, Mandla, Seoni, Itarsi, Mauganj and Sarni with student s trength count approximately reaching 4000 with 500 experienced staffs. We have many teams working in parallel to monitor our performance in all sectors and grievance system provide necessary inputs in all stages of ITI’s to attain next high level possible.
    We are proud to say that we are the first choice amongst the student who wants to pursue their career in skill training. Our passed out batches are working in all major public and private sector making our Institutes proud.
