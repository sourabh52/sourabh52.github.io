QUALITY POLICY & MISSION


Quality Policy

To direct & channalise the energy of every aspiring individual, towards exploring the potential of excellence, to the highest level of application.

Mission

At SHRI VIDHYA GYAN GANGA PVT Industrial Training Institute, we are committed to Develop the youth by equipping them with employable and entrepreneurial skill sets suitable to the industry.

Quality Objectives

  • To introduce at least one skill trade in ITI as per demand of local industry.
  • To expose trainees to at least one industry in the vicinity of ITI.
  • Reduce trainees complaint’s by 1%.
  • Ensure reduction in dropout rate by 2%.
  • Ensure exposure of teaching faculty for adapting to new teaching methodologies.
  • Develop holistic personality of trainees through imparting of soft skills.
  • To be recognized as a finest Institute by excellence training, effective placement and incubation centre.
